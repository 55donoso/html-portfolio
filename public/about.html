About Me

Hi! I'm Edwar Donoso, a self-taught developer and creative builder who enjoys turning ideas into real projects. I believe curiosity is my greatest skill. Whether I'm developing software, designing digital experiences, experimenting with AI, or building creative brands, I enjoy learning by creating.

I don't see programming as just writing code. For me, it's a way to solve problems, automate processes, and bring ideas to life. Every project I build is an opportunity to improve my skills, challenge myself, and create something useful for others.

Outside of software, I'm deeply interested in music, branding, psychology, and entrepreneurship. Those interests influence the way I approach technology—always looking for creative solutions instead of conventional ones. This portfolio is the beginning of a long journey, and every project here represents another step toward becoming a better developer and creator.
